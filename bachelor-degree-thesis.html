Re-naturalization proposal of a Roggia di Thiene section (Santorso, Vicenza)

The paper, after an extensive historical investigation useful to understand the Roggia di Thiene morphological evolution, describes the current ditch state also through an hydraulic modeling that uses real pluviometric data and a point cloud detected with a GPS survey. The second part of the thesis proposes three different morphological solutions for the investigated ditch section with the aim of increase the naturality, promote the biodiversity and reduce the hydraulic risk. These proposals are developed through the description of the land morphology modifications and the hydraulic modeling considering different hydraulic flow scenarios
